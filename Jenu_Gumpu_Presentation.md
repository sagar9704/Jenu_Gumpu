# Jenu-Gumpu Presentation (10 Slides)

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Main Title**: Jenu-Gumpu (ಜೇನು-ಗುಂಪು)
**Subtitle**: Empowering Honey Hunters through Digital Collective Bargaining
**Topic**: Android App Development using GenAI
**Program**: MindMatrix VTU Internship Program

---

## Slide 2: Introduction
**Vision**: Transform tribal and rural honey hunters into brand owners.
*   Mission: Provide a digital platform for harvest management, quality improvement, and fair trade.
*   Target Audience: Forest-dwelling communities and rural beekeepers.
*   Goal: Economic liberation through technology and collective effort.

---

## Slide 3: The Problem Statement
**The Challenges Faced by Producers:**
*   **Exploitation by Middlemen**: Producers often receive only 10-20% of the final retail price.
*   **Information Asymmetry**: Lack of awareness regarding urban market prices and demand.
*   **Quality Standards**: No standardized grading or filtering at the source.
*   **Lack of Traceability**: Difficult to establish a brand for "Forest-to-Table" organic products.

---

## Slide 4: The Solution
**Jenu-Gumpu: The Digital Collective**
*   **Digital Ledger**: Real-time logging of honey collection and floral sources.
*   **Quality Tools**: Standardizing grading through color and moisture check guides.
*   **Collective Strength**: Aggregating small harvests into a large "Collective Stock" for better bargaining power.
*   **Accessibility**: A simple, icon-driven interface with full **Kannada** language support.

---

## Slide 5: Feature: Harvest Log & Collective Stock
*   **Record Details**: Capture Date, Location, Quantity (kg), and Floral Source (e.g., Wildflower, Coffee).
*   **Digital History**: Searchable records of all past harvests to track production trends.
*   **Collective Dashboard**: Real-time view of the total honey stock available within the community group.

---

## Slide 6: Feature: Honey Grading Guide
*   **Visual Standards**: Simplified guides for color and moisture checks.
*   **Accessibility**: Replaces complex text with intuitive icons (Star, Check, Info).
*   **Value Addition**: Helps hunters categorize honey into Grade A, B, or C to demand premium prices for high-quality produce.

---

## Slide 7: Feature: Profit Calculator & Price Monitor
*   **Market Price Monitor**: Real-time comparison between local wholesale and city retail prices.
*   **Logic-based Calculator**: Estimates net earnings after accounting for processing, filtering, and transportation costs.
*   **Data-Driven Decisions**: Empowers producers to decide when and where to sell for maximum profit.

---

## Slide 8: Sustainability & Impact
*   **Sustainable Harvesting**: Educational module on harvesting honey without destroying bee colonies.
*   **Economic Independence**: Improving the livelihoods of tribal communities through fair trade.
*   **Ecological Preservation**: Promoting chemical-free, organic honey collection that protects forest biodiversity.

---

## Slide 9: Technical Implementation
*   **Frontend**: Built with **Jetpack Compose** (Modern Android UI framework).
*   **Language**: Developed using **Kotlin** for robustness and efficiency.
*   **Backend**: Node.js API for collective data management.
*   **GenAI Integration**: Utilizing AI for code generation, UI optimization, and bilingual content generation.

---

## Slide 10: Conclusion & Future Scope
*   **Conclusion**: Jenu-Gumpu is more than an app—it's a tool for economic liberation and forest preservation.
*   **Future Scope**:
    *   Direct-to-consumer e-commerce integration.
    *   Blockchain-based traceability for "Forest-to-Table" certification.
    *   AI-powered honey purity detection using smartphone cameras.
